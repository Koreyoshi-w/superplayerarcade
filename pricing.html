Good to Know

A few things that'll help you plan your visit.

🚶 Walk-ins always welcome

No reservation needed for arcade play or studio activities. Just show up whenever you're ready — our doors are open.

💰 Prices may vary

Studio prices depend on project size and materials chosen. The prices shown are starting points — ask our staff for the current menu when you arrive.

🎁 Everything's included

Studio prices include all materials, supplies, and guidance. You don't need to bring anything — just yourself and maybe some friends.

👥 Groups and events

Planning something for a bigger group? We offer custom packages for team outings, corporate events, and special occasions. Just reach out.

🕐 Hours

Mon–Thu: 12pm–9pm. Fri–Sat: 11am–10pm. Sun: 11am–8pm. Last studio sessions start one hour before close.

📲 Stay up to date

Follow us @superplayerarcade for seasonal specials, new activities, and limited-time pricing. Things change — in a good way.
